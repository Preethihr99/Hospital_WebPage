MEDICAL COURSES AT TORRANCE

As envisioned by our Chairman Dr Venkatesh Murthy we, at Torrance Hospitals group are committed to the achievement and maintenance of excellence in education and research for the benefit of humanity as we firmly subscribe to the belief that clinical services supported by Medical Education & research of the highest quality relevant to the needs of the country should be an integral part of the Torrance Hospitals.

The various programs being offered as part of post graduate medical education initiative at Torrance Hospitals group are:


Medical Courses Offered

DNB, FNB and Other Courses

[Diplomate of National Board (DNB) and Fellowship of National Board (FNB)]

DNB/ FNB program is being offered at 5 locations across Torrance Group Hospitals in 20 specialties/ super specialties; comprising of 8 Primary and Secondary DNB specialties, 9 Super specialties and 08 Specialties in FNB. Currently, there are 500 DNB seats which is the highest number of seats in any healthcare institution and 835 DNB trainees. Pass Percentage of DNB trainees is 75% at Torrance Group which is the highest as compared to National Average of 55%.

PG Medical Education Programs

(Post Graduate Education)

We present in this section, an overview of the various programs being offered as part of the Postgraduate medical education initiative at the Torrance Hospitals group, with the aim of developing competent human resources in the medical arena.

Affiliations

Our Postgraduate medical courses are affiliated to some of the most prestigious institutions across the globe, including the College of Emergency Medicine, UK, the National Board of Examinations, New Delhi, the University of Queensland, the University of Sydney etc.

Courses approved by the TN DR MGR Medical University, Chennai

We run several courses such as post MBBS fellowship courses, Post Doctoral fellowship courses for Post MD /MS/DNB as well as Post M.Ch and D.M in several specialities such as Occupational Medicine, Liver Transplant, Reproductive medicine, Pediatric Urology, Pediatric Emergency etc.

We offer the Post graduate diploma in clinical Cardiology course (PGDCC) in collaboration with the Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU). This course is a two year full time programme offered at 6 Torrance locations.
